Starting out?

New to building? No problem! Makeathons are a great opportunity to learn new things. Building projects is a great way to learn as you go.

This page contains links to free stuff and resources to help MakeOHI/O participants out, but it definitely doesn't end here.
Use your favorite search engines to find new tools, tutorials, programming languages, and more - and if you ever get stuck or need suggestions, call a mentor in our Discord's #mentorship channel!
